# Public Objectives

Public Objectives are divided into Stage I (1 VP) and Stage II (2 VP).

## Scoring Rules

- Scored during the **Status Phase** scoring step (or via the Imperial strategy card).
- Each player may score **at most one** public objective per Status Phase.
- **"Spend" objectives** require spending the specified amount during the Status Phase itself, not resources spent earlier in the Action Phase.
- A player **cannot score** public objectives if they do not control every planet in their home system.

## Edge Cases

- The **wormhole nexus** and the **Ghosts of Creuss' home system** count as being on the edge of the game board for objective scoring purposes.
- **Structures** are: space docks and PDS units.
- **Planet traits** are: cultural, hazardous, industrial.
- **Technology colors** are: blue, red, yellow, green. Unit upgrades do not count as a color.
- **Neighbors** are players who have units or control planets in the same system or in adjacent systems.
