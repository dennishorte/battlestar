# Objectives

## Rules

### Objective Cards

- There are 5 Objective cards. Some are marked "1-3P" or "4P" for specific player counts. Omit any that don't match your player count.
- During setup, shuffle the applicable Objective cards. Each player draws one at random and places it face up in their supply.
- The player whose Objective shows the First Player marker takes that marker.

### Battle Icon Matching

- Each Objective card has a battle icon (Crysknife, Desert Mouse, or Ornithopter).
- Objective cards participate in the battle icon matching system: when you win a Conflict and take the Conflict card into your supply, check if you have another face-up Conflict or Objective card with the same battle icon. If you do, flip the matching pair face down and gain 1 Victory Point.

### First Player Determination

- The first player is determined by Objective cards during setup. The player whose Objective shows the First Player marker takes it.
