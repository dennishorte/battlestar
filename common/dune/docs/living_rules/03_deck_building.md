# Deck Building

## Rules

- You start the game with a 10-card deck containing the same cards as the other players:
  - Convincing Argument (x2)
  - Dagger (x2)
  - Diplomacy (x1)
  - Dune, the Desert Planet (x2)
  - Reconnaissance (x1)
  - Seek Allies (x1)
  - Signet Ring (x1)
- During each round, you may acquire new cards to add to your deck using Persuasion gained during your Reveal turn.
- Whenever you acquire a new Imperium or Reserve card, it is first placed in your discard pile.
- Any time you are unable to draw a card because your deck is empty, reshuffle your discard pile to form a new deck, then continue to draw as needed.
- There are ways to "trash" cards, removing them from your deck altogether for the rest of the game. Strategically removing weaker cards increases the chances of drawing stronger cards more often.
- The effects of each card in your deck are divided into two parts: an Agent box and a Reveal box. On any given turn, you may use the effects contained in only one of those boxes: the Agent box during an Agent turn, or the Reveal box during a Reveal turn.
