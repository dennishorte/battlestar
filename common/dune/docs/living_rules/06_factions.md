# Factions

## Rules

- There are four Factions: Emperor, Spacing Guild, Bene Gesserit, and Fremen.
- Your Faction cubes begin at the bottom of each Faction's Influence track.
- When you send an Agent to a Faction's board space, gain one Influence with that Faction by advancing your cube one space on its Influence track. Other game effects can also move your cube up (and occasionally down) the track.

### Victory Points at 2 Influence

- When you reach 2 Influence with a Faction, you gain a Victory Point.
- If you drop back below 2 Influence, you lose that Victory Point.

### Bonus at 4 Influence

- When you reach 4 Influence, you earn the bonus shown on that space of the track.
- If you drop back below 4 Influence, you do not give back the bonus.
- It is possible (though unusual) to earn the same bonus more than once, if you move back then advance again.

### Alliance at 4 Influence

- The first player to reach 4 Influence with a Faction earns an Alliance with that Faction.
- Take the Alliance token from the track, put it in your supply, and gain the Victory Point shown on the Alliance token.
- If you are ever passed by an opponent rising to a higher space on the track, you must give the Alliance token to that opponent. You lose that Victory Point and the opponent gains it.
