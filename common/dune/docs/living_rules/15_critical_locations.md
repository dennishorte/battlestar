# Critical Locations and the Shield Wall

## Rules

### Control Markers

- Some Conflict cards represent a struggle at one of three locations on Dune: Arrakeen, Spice Refinery, or Imperial Basin (based on the card's title).
- If you win such a Conflict, your rewards include control of the board space: take a Control marker from your supply and place it on the flag below the appropriate space.

### Control Bonuses

- While your Control marker is on one of these spaces, you receive the bonus shown whenever any player (yourself included) sends an Agent there:
  - Arrakeen: 1 Solari
  - Spice Refinery: 1 Solari
  - Imperial Basin: 1 spice

### Defensive Bonus

- When a Conflict card is revealed for a space that you already control, you receive a defensive bonus: you may deploy one troop from your supply to the Conflict.

### Shield Wall

- The three critical locations are protected from storms and sandworms by the Shield Wall, a token placed on the board during setup.
- While the Shield Wall token remains in place, no sandworms can be summoned to a Conflict at one of these three protected locations.
- When the Shield Wall detonation icon appears on a card or board space, you may remove the Shield Wall token from the board (returning it to the box). Once removed, sandworms may be summoned to any Conflict.
