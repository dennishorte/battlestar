# Recall and Endgame (Phase 5)

## Rules

### Endgame Check

- If any player is at 10 or more Victory Points on the Score track, or if the Conflict Deck is empty, the Endgame is triggered.

### Recall (If No One Has Won)

1. Players recall their Agents, returning them to their Leaders.
2. Pass the First Player marker clockwise to the next player.
3. Begin a new round with Phase 1.

### Endgame

1. Each player may play and resolve any Endgame Intrigue cards they have.
2. Whoever has the most Victory Points is declared the winner.

### Tiebreakers

In the case of a tie, tiebreakers are applied in this order:

1. Most spice
2. Most Solari
3. Most water
4. Most garrisoned troops
