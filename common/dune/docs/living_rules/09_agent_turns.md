# Agent Turns

## Rules

Starting with the player who has the First Player marker and continuing clockwise, players take one turn at a time. On your turn, you take either an Agent turn or a Reveal turn.

### Agent Turn Procedure

1. Play one card from your hand face up in front of you.
2. Use that card to send an Agent from your Leader to an unoccupied space on the game board. The board space must have an icon in its upper left corner matching one of the Agent icons on the card.
3. You must pay any costs and meet any requirements of the board space you choose.
4. Gain the effects of the board space as well as the effects in the Agent box of the card you played. If the board space belongs to one of the Factions, also gain one Influence with that Faction. You may carry out all these effects in any order.

### Restrictions

- You must choose only one Agent icon on your card; one card cannot send multiple Agents.
- You cannot send an Agent to a board space that already has one (though Spies can provide a way around this via Infiltrate).
- If a card does not have any Agent icons on it, you may not play it during an Agent turn. It may only be revealed during a Reveal turn.
- You ignore the Reveal box of the card during Agent turns.

### Optional Agent Turns

- Agent turns are optional. You may take a Reveal turn while you still have Agents instead of taking an Agent turn.
- Once you have taken a Reveal turn, your turns are skipped for the rest of the phase.
- Once all players have taken a Reveal turn, Phase 2 ends.

### Plot Intrigue Cards

- You may play any Plot Intrigue cards you have at any point during one of your own Agent or Reveal turns.
