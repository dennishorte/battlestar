# Round Start (Phase 1)

## Rules

1. Reveal a new Conflict card from the top of the Conflict Deck. Place it face up in the space next to the Conflict Deck (on top of any Conflict cards that might remain from previous rounds).
2. Each player draws five cards from their own deck, forming their hand for the round.
